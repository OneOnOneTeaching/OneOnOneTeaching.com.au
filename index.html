ABOUT ONE ON ONE TEACHING

Damien began tutoring with the aim of helping students develop a love for learning.

Experienced and highly qualified, Damien understands that every student has their own unique learning style, strengths, and weaknesses. Therefore at One on One Teaching, Damien personally assesses each student and tailors an approach to meet the individual needs of the student. Damien works closely with the student, providing them with the necessary help and support.

One on One Teaching specialises in one-on-one tutoring and exam preparation. Focusing on Math and English. Damien is experienced and passionate about teaching and helping students.

One of the key features of One on One Teaching is the personalized approach to teaching. Damien believes that every student is unique and requires a customized teaching plan. Damien works closely with students to understand their learning style, strengths, and weaknesses, and designs a program that best suits their individual needs. This approach has helped many students.

One on One Teaching is highly respected in the local Lane Cove Village community providing personalized and high-quality tutoring services to students of all ages.
